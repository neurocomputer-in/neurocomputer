NeuroComputer

AI agents that handle your tasks — from coding to freelancing to browser work

What It Does

NeuroComputer is an AI agent system. You give it a task, it breaks it down into steps and executes them using specialized skills called "neuros." It can code, analyze job postings, control your browser, and more — all through conversation.

No complex setup. No rigid workflows. Just tell it what you need.

What You Can Do

💻 Code Projects

Create files, edit code, manage entire projects. It plans the structure, writes the code, and tracks changes.

📋 Upwork Jobs

Capture job postings, analyze them for fit, and draft proposals — all from one conversation.

🌐 Browser Work

Delegate web tasks to an AI browser agent. Opens sites, fills forms, extracts data automatically.

🛠️ Build New Skills

Create new neuros on the fly during conversation. No restart needed — they're live instantly.

💬 Natural Chat

Talk to it like a colleague. It understands context, remembers your work, and figures out what to do.

🎙️ Voice Support

Talk to your agents. Get spoken responses. Works with your microphone and speakers.
